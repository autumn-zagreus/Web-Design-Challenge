Visualization #2: Latitude vs. Humidity

The image above shows the latitude and humidity of different cities on January 5th, 2017 plotted against one another.

On this particular day, there does not seem to be any strong correlation between the latitude and percent humidity of a given city. Cities of any latitude are shown to have high percentage humidity, but there are also cities of any latitude showing low (or even 0) percent humidity.
