Visualization #4: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The image above shows the latitude and wind speed of different cities on January 5th, 2017 plotted against one another.

There does not seem to be too a strong correlation between city latitude and wind speed on this particular day, however we can see an increase in the highest wind speed as we move away from latitude 0 in either direction, but it is particularly notable at positive latitudes. However, there are also much, much lower wind speeds in either direction from latitude 0.
