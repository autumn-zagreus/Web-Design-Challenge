Visualization #3: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The image above shows the latitude and cloudiness of different cities on January 5th, 2017 plotted against one another.

There does not seem to be any correlation between the latitude of a given city and the percentage cloudiness on that day. Both positive and negative latitudes show low and high percentages of cloudiness.
